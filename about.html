About Me

Hi! I'm Rizul Sharma, a student passionate about exploring the intersections of mathematics, computer science, and human behavior. Since Grade 6, I've been immersed in STEM: from First Lego League, Sci-Tech programs, hackathons, to designing educational STEM lessons and AI research.

I love building projects that are both creative and logical, teaching STEM to younger students, and understanding how the brain reacts to technology and problem-solving situations.
